How we work
Our dehydration process
From fresh produce to shelf-ready dehydrated goods — every step under one roof at our HSIIDC facility.
1
Raw material sourcing
Fresh fruits & vegetables sourced from local farms and mandis
2
Cleaning & sorting
Washing, grading, and removing sub-standard produce
3
Dehydration
Controlled-temperature drying to preserve nutrition and colour
4
Quality check
Moisture content, colour, hygiene, and weight testing
5
Packing & dispatch
Bulk or custom retail packing, then dispatched to buyer
